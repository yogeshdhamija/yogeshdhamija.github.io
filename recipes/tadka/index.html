Tadka

Into a pan on stove:
1 tablespoon Olive Oil

When warm:
1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (jira)

When brown:
1 cups diced Onion
2" piece of root of Ginger (adrak), diced

When brown:
0.5 cups diced Tomato
??? Chilli Pepper

Lower temp, then:
2 teaspoon coriander powder (dhaniya)
1/2 teaspoon garam masaala
2 teaspoon cayenne (laal mirch)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon turmeric (haldi)
Acid (lemon/lime juice)

Higher temp and mix. Add water if too dry.
