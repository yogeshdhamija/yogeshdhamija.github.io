Chai
2020-07-16 18:52

~4 servings.

Into a large pot on stove:
3 cups water
5 tablespoons Assam loose leaf black tea
1 inch ginger (adhrak) sliced
10 cloves (laung)
15 green cardamom pods (elaichi)
2 inch cinnamon (daalchini) stick
15 black peppercorns
5 star anises
0.5 teaspoon fennel seeds (saumph)

When at a low boil:
Cover pot
Lower heat to simmer and let it sit

After at least 10 minutes:
3 cups milk

Source: “The Perfect Indian Masala Chai”, Medium article by Harshad Kulkarni, with modifications.
