Chicken Curry
2021-02-02 18:19

~5 servings.

Into a deep pan on stove:
9 tablespoons oil

When hot, add and constantly stir:
3 bay leaves
12 cloves
6-inch cinnamon piece
9 green cardamom

After about a minute, add and constantly stir:
3 cup onions, finely chopped
6 thai green chilis, slit

When onions are golden, add and constantly stir:
1.5 tablespoons ginger, minced
1.5 tablespoons garlic, minced

After about a minute, add and constantly stir:
1.5 cup tomatoes, finely chopped

When tomatoes mushy, stir in:
1 cup yoghurt
0.75 tsp turmeric
3 tsp cayanne pepper
3 tsp garam masala
3 tsp coriander

When yoghurt smell goes away, stir in:
3 lbs chicken breast, cubed
6 tbsp cilantro leaves, minced

Fry chicken, flipping occasionally, until white (about 3 minutes).

Cover and cook for about 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Stir in:
??? cup hot water (enough to make a thick curry)

Cover and cook on low flame, stirring occasionally, until chicken is soft-cooked.

Source: “Chicken Curry Recipe”, by Swasthi.
