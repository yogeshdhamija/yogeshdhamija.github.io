Chole
2020-07-10 22:28

~5 servings.

Into a pot on stove:
3 cups white chickpeas soaked overnight
??? cups water (enough to barely cover the chickpeas)
3 inch cinnamon stick
15 cardamom pods (elaichi)
1.5 bay leaf
1.5 teaspoon ginger paste
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
1.5 teaspoon salt

After chickpeas cooked al dente:
Strain out water and save it
Remove cinnamon, cardamom pods, bay leaves from chickpeas

Into a large pan on stove:
6 tablespoon oil

When hot:
1.5 large onion chopped

When brown:
6 green chillies slit
5.25 medium-sized tomato chopped
1.5 teaspoon ginger chopped

When soft and mushy:
all the chickpeas obtained previously

After flavours combine (couple minutes):
3 teaspoon cumin powder (jira)
3 teaspoon coriander powder (dhaniya)
1.5 teaspoon cayenne (laal mirch)
3 teaspoon garam masaala
??? teaspoon turmeric (haldi)
lemon squeezed, to taste

After mixing:
until slightly too wet: add stock obtained previously, or water if there’s not enough

Reduce until desired consistency
Add salt to taste

Source: “Amritsari Chole”, by Dassana Amit, with modifications. Image attribution: by Divya Kudua, on Wikimedia Commons. Licence: CC BY.
